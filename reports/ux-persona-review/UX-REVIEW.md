# UX Persona Review

App: Vendingpreneurs admin — SEO Page Builder
URL: http://localhost:3000/admin/pages
Date: 2026-06-04
Pages tested: 6
Screenshots captured: 29
Personas: 15

---

## Executive Summary

- **Total unique issues:** 38
- **P0 — Critical:** 5
- **P1 — High:** 11
- **P2 — Medium:** 15
- **P3 — Low:** 7
- **Blockers (raised by ≥1 persona):** 9 distinct blocking conditions
- **Average gut feel across all 6 pages:** 3.0 / 5

The product has genuinely strong bones — clean rendering (zero console errors, zero failed network requests across all pages), excellent draft-vs-live status language, explicit autosave with timestamps, correct redirect (301/302/307/308) semantics, and a block-variant audit page that several personas praised as a maturity signal. The problem is concentrated almost entirely in the **Page Builder Editor**, which averaged **1.9/5** and was scored 1/5 by six personas: it is an 82-button / 58-input three-pane wall with no onboarding, no focal point, pervasive unexplained CMS/SEO jargon ("eyebrow", "slug", "canonical", "governance", "funnel stage"), no undo, no drag-and-drop, no keyboard shortcuts, a silently-failing clipboard action, and a mobile layout that collapses into an unusable cloud of floating pills. The supporting shell pages (List, Create, Authors, Redirects) are mostly calm and usable; their recurring sins are unexplained jargon, unlabelled status dots, missing empty states, and unmarked required fields. A global floating "N" avatar overlapping the "Sign out" control appears on every shell page.
