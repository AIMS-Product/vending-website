# Persona Review — Betty (Tech-Illiterate Grandparent, 78)

**Lens**: I read every word on the screen because I am frightened of breaking
something. I need every button to say exactly what it does, I need the next step
to be obvious, and when I do something important I need the screen to tell me
clearly, in big plain words, that it worked. If I can't undo a thing, I will not
touch it.

---

## Summary

- **Pages reviewed**: 16 public/admin pages + 8 journeys
- **Findings**: 21
- **Blockers (for me)**: 1 (the apply confirmation is so small I cannot tell I
  succeeded)
- **Overall gut feel**: **2.5 / 5** — The website is handsome and the words are
  mostly kind, but the two things I actually came to do (apply, and know it
  worked) left me unsure I'd done anything at all, and the big "0+" numbers on
  the front page made me wonder if the whole thing was broken.

### Notes per journey

**Discover & Apply** — I filled in my name and all the boxes (the little red
stars told me which ones I had to do, which was kind). But after I pressed
**SUBMIT APPLICATION**, the only thing that changed was one small line of green
writing next to the button: "Thanks. We received your details and will follow up
shortly." (`journey-discover-and-apply-004-04-submit-continue-round-1-.png`). My
eyes were on the big orange button, not off to the right. The boxes I'd typed in
were all still sitting there full. To me that means it didn't go through — so I'd
press the button again. The notes even say the test computer pressed it five more
times for the same reason. There is a whole "Thanks for applying" page in this
website (`thank-you-for-applying-001-load.png`) that would have reassured me, but
the form never took me there.

**Contact the Team** — I looked along the top menu — ABOUT, RESOURCES, CASE
STUDIES, NEWS — and there is no "Contact". I would not have known to scroll right
down to the bottom of the page to find "CONTACT US" in the small grey footer
(`journey-contact-the-team-002`). I'd assume there was no way to reach a person.

**Admin: Create SEO Page** — A "Quick Tour" pop-up appeared and sat right on top
of the panel it was pointing at, so I couldn't press the thing it told me to
press (`journey-admin-create-seo-page-s04-04-wizard-starting-point-build.png`).
That is exactly the sort of thing that makes me afraid I've broken the computer.

## Page-by-Page Review

### / (Home) — gut feel 2/5

The big words are reassuring and the customer stories are lovely and long (I read
them all). But three large numbers say **"0+"**, **"$0M+"**, **"0+"** where I'd
expect real figures (`home-002-full.png`; text: "0+ / Entrepreneurs launched").
To me a zero means nobody has done this — it frightened me off. The top menu also
has no "Home" and no "Contact". On my iPad the page had a sideways scrollbar
(`home-003-mobile.png`, log: "horizontal scroll present"), which always makes me
feel I've zoomed something wrong.

### /apply — gut feel 2/5

The form itself is clear and the red stars help. When I pressed submit with empty
boxes, a little message "Please fill out this field." popped up
(`apply-003-form-empty-submit.png`) — that I understood and liked. My worry is
entirely the success message (see journey above).

### /contact — gut feel 3/5

Clear form. "SEND MESSAGE" is a good plain label. My only trouble is finding the
page at all.

### /news — gut feel 2/5

I clicked **NEWS** in the menu and the page is headed **"BLOG"** in giant letters
(`news-001-load.png`; text: "BLOG"). Two different words for the same thing
confuses me — did I land in the wrong place? And above the headings the article
cards were empty outlines until I scrolled, so at first the page looked broken
and half-loaded.

### /news/how-to-choose… (article) — gut feel 3/5

Readable. But the little crumb trail says "HOME / BLOG / LOCATION SCOUTING"
(`news-how-to-choose-the-perfect-location-for-vending-machine-001-load.png`)
while I got here from "NEWS" — that word "BLOG" again. The list page also failed
to load one of its pictures (log: "500 ... /\_next/image"), which I'd read as the
site being poorly kept.

### /thank-you-for-applying — gut feel 4/5

THIS is the page I wanted to see after applying — big "THANKS FOR APPLYING." and
plain next steps (`thank-you-for-applying-001-load.png`). It's a shame the form
never sends me here.

### /this-page-does-not-exist (404) — gut feel 4/5

"Page not found... The page you're looking for doesn't exist or has moved" with a
"Back to home" button (`this-page-does-not-exist-001-load.png`). Plain and kind —
exactly what a scared person needs.

### /case-studies, /about, /privacy, /terms, /pre-call-resources — gut feel 3/5

All loaded with real, readable content. No trouble, just the same top menu with
no Home/Contact and the small grey links everywhere.

### Admin pages — gut feel 2/5

I am not an admin, but if my grandson asked me to add a news post I'd be lost:
there is no News or Blog link in the side menu at all
(`journey-admin-create-news-draft-s02`), and the actions are hidden behind small
"⋮" three-dot buttons and tiny 10×10 pixel status dots
(log: `button "SEO readiness: Blocked" 10x10px`) that I could never aim at.

## My Top 10 Issues

1. Apply success is a tiny green line, not a page or banner — I can't tell it worked.
2. Homepage shows "0+", "$0M+", "0+" — looks broken and untrustworthy.
3. "Contact" is missing from the top menu — I can't find how to reach a person.
4. "NEWS" in the menu but "BLOG" on the page — same thing, two names, confuses me.
5. News article cards are blank until scrolled — page looks broken on arrival.
6. Admin "Quick Tour" pop-up covers the button it tells me to press.
7. Admin News/Blog section has no menu link — unreachable unless you know the address.
8. Homepage scrolls sideways on my iPad — feels like something's wrong.
9. A picture on the news page fails to load (500 error) — site looks unmaintained.
10. Tiny 10×10px status dots and "⋮" menus in admin are far too small to aim at.
