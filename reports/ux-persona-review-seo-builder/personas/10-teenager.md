# Persona Review — 10. Zoe (Confused Teenager, 16)

> I only know TikTok, Insta, Snap. I've never used a "business app" in my life. If I
> have to read a paragraph to figure out a button, I'm out. This thing made me read
> like a hundred paragraphs and it kept telling me to "fix" stuff that wasn't broken.

## Overall (Zoe)

**2/5.** The published page at the end looks genuinely nice and modern — but getting
there is the opposite of intuitive. The publish flow plays hide-and-seek with what's
broken, the schedule feature gives zero feedback and is on the wrong timezone, drafts
have no undo, and redirects trap your typos forever. Words like "Slug", "301", and
"Governance comments" are exactly the corporate/dev language I bounce off. It doesn't
feel like an app made for someone like me — it feels like software made for someone who
already knows how websites work.
